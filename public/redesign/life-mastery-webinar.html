Frequently Asked Questions

Webinar FAQs

Do I need any special software to attend?

You'll need a computer or mobile device with internet connection. We use Zoom for the webinar, and you'll receive a link after registration. No special software download is required for basic participation.

What if I can't attend live?

All registered attendees receive access to the recording for 30 days after the event, so you can watch it at your convenience.

Will I be able to ask questions?

Yes! There's a dedicated Q&A session at the end of the webinar where you can ask questions directly to our expert coaches.

What materials will I receive?

You'll receive a downloadable workbook, presentation slides, and access to additional resources related to the webinar topic.

Is there a special offer for attendees?

Yes! Webinar attendees receive exclusive discounts on our full programs, including the Life Mastery Bootcamp and certification programs.
